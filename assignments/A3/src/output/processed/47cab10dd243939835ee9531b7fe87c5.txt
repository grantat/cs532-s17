An exciting Piano Solo adventure blending Brazilian Rhythms with Creative Jazz Art Form
While I was traveling through Brazil this year. I felt a deep desire to record a piano solo based on the depths of the Brazilian heartland. From Rio de Janeiro's breathtaking views to Minas Gerais countryside mountains with beautiful waterfalls and forests. I brought the power jazz feels from the city of lights, New York into the tropical nature of Brazil. Sit back, relax and dream with nature's beaches, oceans and sounds.
