From the live DVD "We Like It Here"
Buy It Here:
http://ropeadope99.bandcamp.com/album...
http://itunes.apple.com/us/album/we-l...
Recorded and filmed live (free of overdubs) from October 7-10 at Kytopia Studios in Utrecht, the Netherlands, for GroundUP Music. For more information and upcoming tour dates, please visit
http://groundup.ropeadope.com

Mixed by Eric Hartman in Dallas, Texas.
Mastered by Scott Hull at Masterdisk New York, NY.
