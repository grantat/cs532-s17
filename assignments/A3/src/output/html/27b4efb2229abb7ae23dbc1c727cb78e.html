7:30 PM: A Writer’s Voice Extra: Sojourner Truth: Francesca Rheannon

We examine the life of nineteenth century abolitionist and former slave Sojourner Truth and what she means to us. We talk with Jacqueline Sheehan about her novel about Sojourner Truth, The Comet’s Tale; with historian Nell Irvin Painter, author of Sojourner Truth, A Life, A Symbol; and listen to a portion of the Enchanted Circle Theater musical play, Sojourner’s Truth: I Will Shake Every Place I Go To. A complete version of this program is at www.writersvoice.net/2012/12/sojourner-truth-ma-humanities
