Bootsy Collins with James Brown Live (1971)

by Corey Brown | Bio
Sunday, January 30th, 2011
Email
Comment

Bootsy Collins sent us this video, saying “see 18 yr old Bootsy with James Brown”. The video was shot in April 1971, live in Bologna, Italy.
