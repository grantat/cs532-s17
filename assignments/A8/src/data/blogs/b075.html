Saturday, November 7, 2015

British Shopping Week in Tokyo

Lola's Cup Cakes


Margaret Howell Cafe in Shibuya
Neal's Yard Stuff smells so good!

British Shopping Week is being held here in Tokyo, by The British Embassy and Lula Magazine. The members shops are offering the freebies or a special service. Last week I visited some of them in Shibuya and Harajyuku. One of the shops, Lola's Cupcakes from London recently has opened Tokyo store. The cupcakes are not too sweet and very light from all of the whipping. I have tried a lot of cupcakes, and these are by far my favorite! They are moist and flavorful, and the frosting is the best I have ever had. You may not wait in line for hours on weekday. Great for a little post shopping treat but on Saturdays there are a lot of people waiting. #SiGB2015

A Different Light

Last month friends and I visit Hayama which is a beautiful seacoast town facing the Pacific Ocean. It's near one of the most famous tourist areas, Kamakura. The surrounding area has many good restaurants and Cafes. I'm not a person who likes to read Kinfolk magazine though. But I think this is a kind of Kinfolk believer's place.
