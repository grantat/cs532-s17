About Our Show

The Campus Buzz is a specialty show on 89.5 WSOU, Seton Hall's Pirate Radio. Tune in on Sunday nights 8-11pm EST to hear indie classics like The Killers, Pavement, Modest Mouse, Death Cab for Cutie, Bon Iver, Gaslight Anthem, The White Stripes and countless other artists! Listen as well for tracks from bands like Local Natives, We Were Promised Jetpacks, CHRVCHES, Cage The Elephant, The National, and more!

The show is hosted by G-Man while Stetzosonic remains on hiatus. The Campus Buzz is your home for indie, alternative, college rock, and indie-pop music, so if you like any of that, you're sure to enjoy the show!
