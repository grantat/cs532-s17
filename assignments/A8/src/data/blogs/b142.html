Im beginnig to think we are not compatible..

Those were the words that left his mouth after I took a seat at the dinner table last night.

And instantly, without missing a beat, I agreed.

The fact of the matter is that we are compatible, and we do make each other happy; but I have grown accustomed to pushing people away. I don't know why, and I don't even realize I'm doing it until Ive been doing it for a little while.

Why am I afraid? Afraid to love, afraid to be loved? Afraid to let someone see the dark sides of my soul.

I am starting to realize that I am a habitual "rebounder" as well. When things start to get bad, or end..I find myself thinking "onto the next one"
Well, with that being said its not like I stay in relationships. I have been "single" for a while.. I mean since Josh and I and that was Septemberish. September being when I just disconnected myself from the relationship. When the words he spewed when he was angry just cut me too deep. When forgive and forget just was not something I could continue doing anymore.

I am good, I am great. I am capable of loving and being loved in return. I love myself. I love my children. I love the sunny days, and the morning dew. I love the way rain feels when it hits your face. I love helping others who cannot help theirs elves. There is so much I love, why not love someone like I love those things?

Sunday, March 12, 2017

Count your blessings everyday..

Today is Sunday. It is just before noon and I am sitting here at the kitchen table while J is making biscuits and gravy. I made a comment about how nice it would be to have some and he ran to Kroger and got the stuff to make it. The man can cook..Its nice, because I have never been with someone who cooks.
Yesterday was rough, I woke up with a migraine. I felt it coming on Friday night and was able to fight it off, or keep it at bay with different meds. Then Saturday morning it busted through with a vengeance. It was tough because I had kept my friends two year old son for the night and needed to wake up with him, J did, without even giving it a second thought. Then my daughter needed to be picked up from a birthday sleepover. By that time the migraine was so bad I began to fight back the tears. I just laid in the dark room holding my head. J said he would go get her. I spent the entire day in bed. Literally. I finally got up around 8pm to a migraine hangover. I found him downstairs cooking dinner. I thought for sure he would have gone home by then. When I asked him why he was still there he said he couldn't leave me like that. Be still my beating heart...
He has been here everyday for a week, well.. actually, he has been here for 12 nights out of the last 14. I don't get the way I feel about him. I really don't, I often times begin to question the validity of our feelings for each other. I told him one day that he needs to realize I am a wild one, he asked me what I meant by that, and I told him that I was a hurricane wrapped up in a tiny body, he said that I was a free spirit and he didn't have a cages. He lets me be who I am, even celebrates it at times.
