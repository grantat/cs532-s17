SABA - GPS (feat. Twista)


 

Chicago pride shines through on Saba's Bucket List Project. 
Most notably on track "GPS". Not only does the track get a Chi-town legend appearance from Twista, but it also beams with city references that weave in and out of 
the track like a speeding car on an lake shore drive.

LEV: WHITNEY SAYS


Dark, moody, smooth sounds from new indie artist LEV released her first single off of the debut EP "Vomit" on October 14th. Working with Chicago artist and producer Third Wall, LEV's single "Whitney Says" hits all them good feels this fall season! 

The sensual tone is set with atmospheric syths and highlighted by bass notes, all tied together by dripping vocals giving this song an ominous, yet surprisingly sexy sound.

Check it'

BEVERLY

Saturday night the gypsies danced our asses off at Chicago's 
Bottom Lounge to the incredible band that I will never  get enough of,
  The Drums.

Putting on one of the best performances I've ever seen, The Drums did not let down the devoted fans grooving along to the band's indie pop tunes and loving every second of Jonny Pierce's signature moves at the sold out show.
What we didn't expect was to fall in love with the supporting band, Beverly. This Brooklyn lazy rock three piece had the crowed glued to the stage as they wailed their haunting drug rock and hazy vocals starting the night off right. Killin' it!

Check it'
