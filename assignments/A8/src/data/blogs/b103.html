A Few Little Treats

I've got into a bit of a bad good habit over the last few months of going out for food and cocktails (and seemingly sometimes milkshakes) with friends and family a bit too often, but I'm not going to apologise for spending (way more money than can be justified on food and drink) time with the people I love! I think it's been all the lovely sunshine and hot weather recently, it just makes me want to get outside and enjoy it. So here's a few pics from my trips out - there's literally only 3 because you'll be glad to know that I'm not one of those people who has a photoshoot with every piece of food they ever eat.
Black Milk Cereal Kult in Affleck's Palace ft. my best friend hiding in the back!
Mine was the chocolate one on the right and it was gorgeous.
Cocktails (and in my Mum's case mocktails) at All Bar One - we'd never been before and just decided last minute that we'd try it out but it was so lovely inside and the drinks were amazing. Ooh and I'd also seriously recommended their chocolate brownie with ice cream and salted caramel cheesecake - I don't think I need to explain why.
Another one from inside All Bar One, I loved the decor inside especially these lights - and also on the wall behind is actually a massive map of the north of England which I thought was a really cool touch.

I do have other pictures but they're mainly very unflattering drunk selfies and shenanigans that I don't really think anyone wants to see! Haha
