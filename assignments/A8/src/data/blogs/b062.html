Vinyl Spins - Roches

Went upstairs to listen to the first Roches album from 1979 and mourn Maggie Roche who passed away earlier this year at age 65.  I loved the sweet and unusual harmonies of the three Roche sisters. We saw them perform at the old UB Norton Hall in 1980 or 81. I have most of their group albums and many of their various solo and pair albums too. They were fun and the singing sounds so warm on vinyl. Their last album was the wonderful Moonswept released in 2007.
