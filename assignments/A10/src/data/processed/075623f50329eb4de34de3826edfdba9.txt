More information
70vibe-fm music is not for every ear but for those who do appreciate its deliverance! Our mission is to keep the seventies musical artistry alive, remembering and featuring most of the music that was published in the seventies. Our playlists include all genres, may it be funk, disco, punk, pop, progressive or psychedelic rock, Jazz, country, folk, which many performing artists and groups were known for in this era.
