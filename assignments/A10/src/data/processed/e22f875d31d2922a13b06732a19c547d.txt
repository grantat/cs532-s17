About Helen
Helen Coleman is a sought after soloist and recording artist who specialises in performing, singing and teaching Classical Music, Jazz and Blues, Musical Theatre and Contemporary Classical music.  Having performed nationally and internationally, Helen now resides in the Scenic Rim area, and travels to perform and record throughout Australia, as well as teaching and holding masterclasses in remote rural Australia.
