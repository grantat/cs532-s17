The Small Faces,-'Come On Children', taken from their first album for Decca,'Small Faces'. This Actual recording is from my first pressed copy of the mono vinyl, which I purchased on it's release date, in 1966.Steve Marriott has one of, the best voices,past and present. I still prefer Steve's soulful voice, than Robert Plant's, Marriott sings it like he means it. Steve Marriott, is a great loss to rock music, and his music will always shine, here on Earth, and in 'Rock Heaven'.
